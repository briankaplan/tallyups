Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2025

Introduction

Tallyups ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal receipt management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

When you use Tallyups, we may collect:

  • Email Data: With your permission, we access your Gmail account to scan for receipt emails. We only read emails that match receipt patterns.
  • Receipt Information: Merchant names, amounts, dates, and receipt images extracted from your emails.
  • Transaction Data: Financial transaction data you import for receipt matching.
  • Contact Information: Your email address for authentication purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Scan your email for receipts and organize them
  • Match receipts to your financial transactions
  • Generate expense reports
  • Improve our receipt detection algorithms

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Encrypted database connections (MySQL with SSL)
  • Secure cloud storage (Cloudflare R2) for receipt images
  • OAuth 2.0 for Gmail authentication (we never see your password)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gmail API: To access your receipt emails with your permission
  • Cloudflare R2: For secure image storage
  • Railway: For application hosting

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our service (under strict confidentiality)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Gmail access at any time via Google Account Settings
  • Export your data

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
